⚙ Powered by Cobalt (open-source)

This tool uses the public cobalt.tools API — a free, open-source, privacy-respecting downloader backend maintained by the community. It returns real, direct download links. No tracking, no ads, no sign-up.

If downloads fail: The video may be private, age-restricted, or geo-blocked. YouTube age-restricted videos won't work. You can also try cobalt.tools directly in your browser. For bulk or advanced use, consider self-hosting cobalt.

⚠ For personal use only. Only download content you own or have explicit permission to download. Respect each platform's Terms of Service and applicable copyright laws.
